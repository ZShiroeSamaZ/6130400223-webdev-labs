The statics taken from Worldmeters website for the first top three that has the most number of cases are shown in section Statics

Most common symptoms:

  • fever
  • dry cough
  • tiredness

Less common symptoms:

  • aches and pains
  • sore throat
  • headache
  • loss of taste or smell
